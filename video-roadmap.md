## Recording notes

- **Length:** aim for 12 to 18 minutes per video. The exam is concept-heavy, so favour clear explanation over long live-coding.
- **Structure each video:** (1) what the domain covers and its exam weight; (2) the core concept demonstrated against the matching lab repo; (3) one common mistake and how to avoid it; (4) a one-screen recap that mirrors the matching cheat sheet.
- **Lab alignment:** screen-record against the live sibling repo so viewers can follow along by cloning it. Keep the on-screen commands identical to the lab `README.md`.
- **British English** in titles, captions and on-screen text (organise, optimise, behaviour, licence). No em or en dashes in captions - use a spaced hyphen.
- **Accessibility:** burn in or upload captions; describe diagrams aloud; keep colour contrast at WCAG AA for any on-screen text.
- **Consistency:** open every video with the course tagline - "Build, supervise, and govern autonomous agents in GitHub-driven delivery." End with a pointer to the mock exam at certy.pro.
- **Publishing:** once a video is live, replace `pending` in the table above with the canonical URL and add it to [`resources/README.md`](./resources/README.md).
